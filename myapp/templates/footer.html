About Us

A Vehicle Management System (VMS) is a software solution designed to streamline and automate the processes related to managing a fleet of vehicles. It is primarily used by organizations, businesses, or government agencies that need to monitor, track, and maintain their vehicles effectively. The system helps in optimizing the utilization of vehicles, improving safety, reducing costs, and ensuring compliance with regulations..
